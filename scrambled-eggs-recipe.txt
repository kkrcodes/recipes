Ingredients:
 - 2 eggs
 - 1 teaspoon of butter
 - 1/2 teaspoon of oil
 - 1/2 teaspoon of salt
 - 1/2 teaspoon of turmeric
 - 1 teaspoon of pepper

Directions:
1. Preheat frying pan/tawa for 2-3 minutes.

2. Beat the eggs nicely into a medium bowl.

3. Add the oil, salt, and turmeric into the bowl and beat until blended.

4. Place the butter on the pan and let it melt.

5. Pour beaten egg mix on the pan and spread it evenly.

6. Let the eggs become semi-solid, and scramble it to expose the liquid eggs.

7. Scramble until eggs look yellow and solid. Do not cook until they're brown.

8. Add pepper to garnish the eggs.

9. Serve with sauce for flavor.
